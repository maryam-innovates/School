About Us

Govt. Tahir Model Girls High School is dedicated to empowering young women and providing high-quality education in the heart of the city. Our mission is to eliminate illiteracy and foster a love for learning among our students.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for future success in various fields. Our academic programs include Mathematics, Sciences, Humanities, and more.

Student Life

At Govt. Tahir Model Girls High School, students actively engage in extracurricular activities such as Girl Guiding and the Menna Bazaar event held annually. These activities promote leadership, teamwork, and creativity.
