About Us

Govt. Tahir Model Girls High School is a public educational institution located in Shalimar Town, Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. The school is dedicated to providing quality education to female students and operates under the Lahore Board. The institution aims to empower young girls through education, fostering an environment conducive to learning and personal growth.

Academics

The school offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet the educational needs of its students.

  • Science
  • Mathematics
  • Social Studies
  • Urdu
  • Islamic Studies

    • The school emphasizes academic excellence and prepares students for board examinations.

    Student Life

    Student life at Govt. Tahir Model Girls High School is enriched with various activities aimed at holistic development.

  • Cultural Events: Celebrations of national days and cultural festivals.
  • Sports: Participation in various sports, although specific facilities may be limited.
  • Clubs and Societies: Opportunities for students to engage in clubs that promote leadership and teamwork.

    • The school environment encourages students to develop their skills and interests outside of the classroom.
