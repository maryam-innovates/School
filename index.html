Frequently Asked Questions

Is GGHS Tahir Model a government or private school?

Yes, GGHS Tahir Model is a government school, operating under the regulations and curriculum set by the Government of Punjab.

What facilities are available at the school?

Our school provides essential facilities including drinking water, electricity, and a computer lab.

Is there an entry test for admission?

Yes, an entry test is conducted. It is important to pass the test to secure admission.

Is GGHS Tahir Model an all-girls school?

Yes, it is an all-girls school dedicated to providing quality education for female students.

Is the teaching staff all female?

Yes, since GGHS Tahir Model is an all-girls school, the teaching staff is composed entirely of female educators.
