About Us

Govt. Tahir Model Girls High School, established in 1992, offers a quality education for girls in Lahore. We emphasize academic excellence, personal development, and community service.

Academics

We offer a variety of programs aimed at developing strong academic foundations. Our curriculum ensures that students are well-prepared for university and beyond.

Student Life

Our school offers numerous extracurricular activities that help students grow in leadership, sports, and the arts, along with opportunities to participate in community service.
